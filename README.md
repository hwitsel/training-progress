# Training Progress

A simple, private app for tracking your strength-training over time — plan your programs, log each workout, and watch your progress build session after session.

## What it's for

Training Progress helps you answer "am I getting stronger?" without spreadsheets or accounts. You set up your training programs, log your sets as you go, and the app turns that into clear progress charts, personal-record highlights, a training calendar, and body-metric trends — all stored entirely on your own device.

## Get it

- **Android** — Play Store *(coming soon)*
- **iOS** — App Store *(coming soon)* 

## Features

- **Programs & exercises** — build training programs, add exercises, and log either a single working value (Max) or per-set (weight × reps), with your own custom exercises.
- **Progress at a glance** — per-exercise charts, sparklines, automatic personal-record (PR) detection, full history, and an adjustable session window.
- **Active session logging** — log a workout as you train, with a built-in rest timer.
- **Edit & fix history** — edit or delete any saved session after the fact.
- **Train Days** — a month calendar coloured by the program you trained each day, with quick-start / repeat for your last session.
- **Body metrics** — track check-ins like body weight and see the signed change over time.
- **Private by design** — fully offline: no account, no network, no analytics, no ads. Your data never leaves your device. See the [Privacy Policy](docs/privacy-policy.md).

## Platforms

- **Android** (this repository) — Kotlin, Jetpack Compose (Material 3), Room, Hilt, type-safe Navigation Compose, with a custom Canvas progress chart. minSdk 26 / targetSdk 35.
- **iOS** — a companion version built with SwiftUI and SwiftData (plus a home-screen widget).
