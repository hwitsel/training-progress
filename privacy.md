# Privacy Policy — Training Progress

**Effective date:** 3 June 2026
**Developer:** Hugo Witsel
**Contact:** waddenwaterlevel@gmail.com

This policy applies to the **Training Progress** app on both platforms:

- **Android** — package `nl.hugowitsel.trainingprogress`
- **iOS** — bundle ID `nl.training.progress.TrainingProgress` (including its home-screen widget)

The two versions behave the same way for privacy purposes.

## Summary

Training Progress is an offline, on-device app. Everything you enter — your training programs, exercises, logged sets (weights and repetitions), body-metric check-ins (such as body weight), and dates — is stored **only on your device**. The app does **not** collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data, and it has no user accounts, no servers, no cloud sync, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

## Information the app handles

The only information in the app is what **you** enter to track your training:

- Training programs and the exercises in them.
- Logged workout data (e.g. weights, repetitions, completion, timestamps).
- Body-metric check-ins (e.g. body weight) and their dates.

This information is stored locally in the app's private storage on your device (Room on Android, SwiftData on iOS) and is used solely to display your history, progress, charts, and calendars inside the app. On iOS, the optional home-screen widget reads this same local data only to show it on your device.

## Information the app does NOT collect

- **No personal account or sign-in.** The app does not ask for your name, email, or any identifier.
- **No network transmission and no cloud sync.** The app does not access the internet and does not send your data off your device. There are no backend servers operated for this app, and the iOS version does **not** sync your data to iCloud/CloudKit.
- **No analytics or tracking.** The app contains no analytics, telemetry, advertising, or crash-reporting components, and does not track your activity across apps or websites.
- **No sharing with third parties.** Because nothing leaves your device, no data is shared with or sold to anyone.

## Permissions and device features

- **Android:** the app uses only the **Vibrate** permission, solely to provide a haptic buzz when the in-app rest timer finishes. It requests no other permissions and no internet/network permission.
- **iOS:** the app does not request access to any personal data sources (no Location, Contacts, Health, Camera, Photos, etc.). If you choose to use the rest-timer reminder, iOS may ask your permission to show **local notifications** and to use haptics; these run entirely on your device and involve no data collection or transmission.

## Device backups

The app stores its data in the standard on-device location, so your data may be included in your device's **own** system backup if you have backups enabled:

- **Android:** your data may be part of your Google account device backup.
- **iOS:** your data may be part of your iCloud or computer (Finder/iTunes) backup.

In both cases this is handled entirely by your operating system and your own account settings — it is **not** a transfer to the developer, and the developer has no access to it. You can control or disable this in your device's backup settings.

## Data retention and deletion

Your data stays on your device for as long as the app is installed. **Uninstalling the app deletes its local data.** You can also remove individual programs, sessions, or metrics within the app at any time. Because the developer never receives your data, there is nothing for the developer to retain or delete on your behalf.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children and collects no personal data from anyone. It contains no ads and no data collection.
